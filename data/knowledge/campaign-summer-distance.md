# Summer Distance Campaign

The Summer Distance campaign focuses on marathon training, breathable apparel, hydration, and recovery.

Hero products:

- AeroStride Marathon Trainer
- Momentum Hydration Vest
- CoreFlex Training Tee
- Pulse Recovery Slides

Messaging priorities:

- Help runners prepare for long, hot training blocks.
- Recommend hydration and recovery products when a customer is already considering marathon shoes.
- Keep upsell suggestions practical and context aware.
