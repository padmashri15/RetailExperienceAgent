# Customer FAQ

## Shipping

Standard shipping usually takes 3 to 5 business days after order processing. Express options may be available at checkout depending on destination and inventory location.

## Returns

Unused products can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Worn footwear and apparel may be eligible for exchange if a product defect is confirmed by support.

## Loyalty

Members earn points on eligible purchases, receive early access to seasonal capsules, and can save preference profiles for better recommendations.

## Promotions

Promotions vary by campaign. Associates should only mention a promotion when it is present in approved campaign materials or returned by a promotion service.
