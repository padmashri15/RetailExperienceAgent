# Returns and Warranty Policy

Customers can start a return request from their order details page or through customer support.

Return windows:

- Standard returns: 30 days from delivery for unused products.
- Defect review: available after the return window when the concern is product quality or workmanship.
- Final-sale items: not eligible for standard returns unless required by law.

Warranty language:

Aster & Ridge reviews manufacturing defects, material failures, and workmanship concerns. The team does not guarantee outcomes before inspection.
