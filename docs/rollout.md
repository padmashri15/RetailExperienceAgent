# Production Rollout Plan

## Phase 1 - Internal Pilot

- Connect staging product, inventory, cart, order, CRM, and loyalty services.
- Ingest approved brand, FAQ, policy, campaign, blog, and customer story content.
- Run evals against common retail journeys and support questions.
- Review all escalation flows with support and legal stakeholders.

## Phase 2 - Limited Customer Beta

- Release to a small traffic percentage.
- Measure conversion rate, AOV, CSAT, containment, escalation rate, and response latency.
- Review unanswered questions and content gaps daily.
- Add dashboard alerts for unsupported claims, missing citations, and checkout drop-offs.

## Phase 3 - Full Launch

- Expand traffic by category and region.
- Add personalization from authenticated customer history.
- Run A/B tests for recommendation strategy and cross-sell timing.
- Harden operational playbooks for incidents, policy changes, and campaign updates.

## Phase 4 - Optimization

- Add automated retrieval evals for each knowledge update.
- Add human quality review for sampled conversations.
- Tune tool descriptions, product ranking, and journey-stage messaging.
- Connect attribution reporting to marketing and commerce analytics.
